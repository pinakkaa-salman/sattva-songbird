FAQs

Where is Sampoojitha Heights located?-

Sampoojitha Heights is strategically located in Belathur, Bengaluru, a fast-developing residential hub near Whitefield. The project offers excellent connectivity to major IT parks, schools, healthcare centres, and entertainment zones — making it an ideal choice for both working professionals and families.

What types of apartments are available at Sampoojitha Heights?-

The project features thoughtfully designed 2 & 3 BHK premium apartments that combine style, comfort, and privacy. With no common walls, each home ensures abundant natural light, ventilation, and a serene living experience.

How many units are there in Sampoojitha Heights?+

Sampoojitha Heights offers 100 exclusive units spread across 1.15 acres of well-planned space, ensuring a boutique community experience with modern amenities and open green areas.

What amenities are available for residents?+

Residents at Sampoojitha Heights can enjoy an exclusive clubhouse, landscaped gardens, indoor and outdoor recreation areas, and other lifestyle amenities designed to promote wellness, leisure, and community living.

Is Sampoojitha Heights a RERA-approved project?-

Yes, Sampoojitha Heights is a RERA-registered project. Buyers can have complete peace of mind regarding transparency, timelines, and compliance as per RERA guidelines.

Is Mysore Road a good location to buy or invest in a new apartment?+

Yes, Mysore Road is one of the sought-after locations in South Bengaluru. With minimal traffic, excellent connectivity the upcoming Rowhouse on Mysore Road are certainly high in demand.
